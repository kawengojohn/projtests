How do I secure my phone and laptop?

Never leave your device, that is, phone or Laptop with anyone you don't know, otherwise your tears will do their work when coming back.

How do I save time?

Avoid irrelevant movements, irrelevant groupings and chats which won't benefit you or anyone. Do something or be involved in situations which build up your life not destroying it.

Should I study all the time?

No, have time to take breaks, have time to pray for yourself and anyone including your enemies, visit your friends and family, have time to talk to them on phone, rest and relax.

How should I study?

People are different and you are yourself. So have time to discover yourself and what works best for you.
